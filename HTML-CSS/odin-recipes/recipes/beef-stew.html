Beef Stew Recipe

World's Best Savory Beef Stew

Savor the comforting taste of a hearty Beef Stew, featuring tender chunks of beef simmered with aromatic herbs and vegetables, creating a rich and flavorful one-pot meal

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil, divided
  • ¼ cup flour
  • ½ teaspoon paprika
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon pepper
  • ¼ teaspoon cumin
  • ¼ teaspoon dried Mexican oregano
  • ¼ teaspoon garlic powder
  • 2 pounds beef chuck roast, cubed
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed and minced
  • 2 (14.5 ounce) cans Hunt's® Diced Tomatoes, divided
  • 1 quart beef broth
  • 2 bay leaves
  • ½ teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes, or to taste
  • 3 large red potatoes, quartered
  • 2 large carrots, peeled and sliced
  • 2 cups frozen corn
  • ½ onion, diced
  • ½ head cabbage, coarsely chopped
